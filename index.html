About Us

GoldenEscapes was founded on the belief that age should never be a barrier to adventure and discovery. Our founders, experienced travel enthusiasts and compassionate caregivers, saw the need for travel services that prioritize comfort, accessibility, and personalized care for seniors. We understand that traveling in later years comes with its own set of challenges, and we are here to ensure that every journey is smooth, safe, and full of joy.

popular destinations

GoldenEscapes is dedicated to providing unforgettable vacation experiences specifically designed for older adults. We understand the unique needs and desires of seniors, and our mission is to create safe, comfortable, and enriching travel opportunities that cater to these needs.

Masaai mara

The Maasai Mara is the site of the world-famous Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and other herbivores move in search of greener pastures

L.Nakuru

Lake Nakuru is renowned for its impressive birdlife, especially the large flocks of flamingos that turn the shoreline pink. It's a bird-watcher's paradise with over 450 bird species, providing a serene and captivating experience for nature lovers.

Samburu

Samburu is home to a variety of species not commonly found in other Kenyan parks, often referred to as the Samburu Special Five: Grevy's zebra, Somali ostrich, reticulated giraffe, gerenuk, and beisa oryx.

Mt Kenya

Mombasa is famous for its stunning white-sand beaches and crystal-clear waters. Popular beaches like Diani, Nyali, and Bamburi provide excellent opportunities for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports, offering a perfect setting for relaxation and enjoyment.

mombasa north coast

Mount Kenya is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, including lush forests, alpine meadows, and dramatic peaks. The scenery is diverse and spectacular, providing ample opportunities for photography and nature appreciation
